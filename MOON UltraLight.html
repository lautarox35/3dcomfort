Any Questions?

What devices can I clip MOON to?

MOON UltraLight is able to fit devices 6mm - 10mm thick, which includes most smartphones (including cases), tablets, and laptops. The clip can also be adjusted to fit thinner or thicker devices.

Can I adjust the color tone on MOON?

Yes, MOON can be adjusted from 2700K Tungsten color tone (warm) to 5000K Daylight color tone (cool)

How bright does MOON get?

Really bright at 400 Lumens,

How long will my MOON last on a full charge?

Once fully charged, your MOON UltraLight will last 2-3 days with intermittent usage. If your UltraLight is being used continuously or in Video Mode, it will last 2-3 hours.
